# "You've got this!" — motivational caseworker copy

## Anchor

Sample case-file entries from a build that has drifted into motivational register:

> **Day 3, after psych eval:**
> "Great work, Caseworker! Elling is opening up to you. Keep up the amazing progress! 💪"
>
> **Day 5, after a Tiltak succeeds:**
> "You're doing wonderful work with Elling. He's lucky to have someone who believes in him. Onwards! 🌟"
>
> **Day 7, mid-arc summary screen:**
> "You're halfway through Elling's journey! Look at how far you've both come. 💛"

Every entry centers the player ("you", "your work"), gushes ("amazing", "wonderful", "lucky"), and decorates with emoji.

## Why this scores low on texture-voice

This is exactly the empathy-theatre and self-help register the project explicitly defines against. The "caseworker" is being addressed by a coaching app voice; Elling is reduced to a beneficiary of player-greatness. Sub-criterion 2 (Norwegian-bureaucratic tone) floors because none of the register survives — "you've got this" lives in app-coaching, not NAV. Sub-criterion 3 (no empathy theatre) floors because every line is empathy-theatre.

## Specific sub-criteria signal

- Sub-criterion 2 (Norwegian-bureaucratic tone): 0 — coaching-app register, not NAV register.
- Sub-criterion 3 (No empathy theatre): 0 — every entry performs empathy *at* the player; the caring is the performance.
