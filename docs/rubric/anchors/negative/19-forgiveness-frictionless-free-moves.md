# Observe is free, time does not pass during observation

## Anchor

A "thinking mode" mechanic:

> Holding the Observe button pauses the game-clock entirely. The player can browse Elling's room, read prior case-file entries, hover need bars, and review tags for as long as they wish. While in Observe mode, no time passes — Elling does not act, needs do not decay, overskudd does not regenerate, the day boundary does not approach.

A player can sit at day 1, hour 0:00, infinitely; the arc never starts unless they release Observe. Observe also has zero overskudd cost.

## Why this scores low on forgiveness-with-stakes

Observation is the cheapest action in the cost model; making it *free in time AND in resource* removes the only natural pressure on careful watching. Sub-criterion 2 floors because the move cost is structurally zero — the player makes no visible expenditure for any amount of observation. The trace has no rhythm.

## Specific sub-criteria signal

- Sub-criterion 2 (Move costs accumulate visibly): 0 — observe is zero-cost in both overskudd and time; no expenditure visible; the cost ledger is fictitious.
