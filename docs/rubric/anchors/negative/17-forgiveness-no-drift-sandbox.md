# Needs don't decay — no urgency, sandbox feel

## Anchor

A retuning where need decay is zero:

> All four `needs.*` channels: drain rate 0.00 / game-hour. Needs hold at their session-start values forever unless explicitly altered by a Tiltak.

A 10-day "do nothing" trace:
- Day 1 needs.hunger: 0.95.
- Day 10 needs.hunger: 0.95.

Elling experiences no biological time. His mother's apologies never appear in the trace; the kitchen window never cracks at night; nothing happens to him that the player did not cause. The arc is a sandbox with no clock pressure.

## Why this scores low on forgiveness-with-stakes

The drift-if-ignored signal IS the cost of inaction. Without it, the player can spend any number of days "thinking" without consequence — but the consequences of inaction are what give the player's eventual decision its texture. Sub-criterion 3 floors because no drift is ever felt; the citizen exists outside of time.

- Sub-criterion 3 (Drift if ignored): 0 — zero-decay rates eliminate the cost of inaction; the arc is a frozen sandbox.
